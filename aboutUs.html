About Us!

The Wandering Wannabees formed in the late summer of 2020 during the COVID-19 outbreak. In the beginning we started out as just a three man acoustic band with Ási on the bass, Jón on guitar and Kevin on guitar, mandolin and harmonica. In 2022 we decided to start performing not only acoustically but with drums. In 2023 we added our fourth member, Heiðrún as our lead female vocalist.


We are currently working on our first single for a release goal on Spotify in the summer of 2024.


Band Members
  • Ási - Bass, Backup Vocals
  • Jón - Guitar, Lead Vocals
  • Heiðrún - Lead Vocals, Percussion
  • Kevin - Guitar, Mandolin, Harmonica, Drums, Lead Vocals
